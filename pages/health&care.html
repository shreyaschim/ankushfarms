health & care


A supply of clean, fresh drinking water is available for Pigs & Goats at all time. Feed, suitable for their age and breeds, is being provided to them as well as balanced diet and Veterinary care is also been provided in our Farms.

Pigs & Goats have regular checkup from a vet to make sure that they are in good health.

Pet pigs are prone to some specific health problems, including:
- dry, scaly skin
- arthritis and lameness, particularly in older animals
- pot-bellied pigs can suffer from inverted eyelids.

Vaccinations:
- Consider vaccinating pigs against diseases such as Erysipelas (every six months), Parvovirus, Escherichia coli, Clostridial infection and various parasitic agents, such as mange, lungworm and lice.
