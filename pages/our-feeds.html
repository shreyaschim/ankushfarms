Our Feeds


Pigs :-

The majority of swine feed starts with ground corn, wheat (middling, flour), and soy (typically meal). These are the protein sources your pig needs to build muscle, and pigs have a whole bunch of muscle. These ingredients are mainly in food -Ground Corn, Wheat Flour, Dehulled Soybean Meal, Cane Molasses, Soybean Oil, and Calcium Carbonate.

Pigs are normally fed twice a day but the amount and type of feed depends on the age and the reproductive state of the pig.

Feeding Chart For Pigs

Pounds Daily Amount of Food

Goats :-

Like the food of other domestic animals, goat feed should contain proper amount of protein, fat, minerals and vitamins.

Goats usually drink less water than other livestock animals. Always try to provide them fresh and clean water. The types of goat feed are described below.
