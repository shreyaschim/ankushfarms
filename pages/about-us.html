About us


The Ankushfarm was started with the intent of supplying high quality breed of pig and goat at affordable prices, our farm has been designed keeping in view various aspects for healthy pork and meat production.

Our pigs are our living, so looking after their welfare of pig and their awareness in digital world is not only the way thing to do, it makes good business sense

The farm has been owned by Ankush Dahake since 2010 and is now run by himself and his team, with the help of their dedicated staff and volunteers.


We are dealing in these categories of pigs:-

1) Australian Yorkshire
2) Large White Yourkshire
3) Middle White Yourkshire
4) Duroc Breed
5) Landrace Breed

Ankushfarm spread over an area of 10000 sq. ft. facility exclusively for pigs and goat. We offer a well facility, veterinarian services, and long distance transportation services. We are recognized nationally as experts in the care, training and understanding the behaviours of keeping pigs.
